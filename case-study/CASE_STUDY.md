## Scenario 1: Cost Allocation and Tracking

**Situation**: The company needs to track and allocate costs accurately across different Warehouses and Stores. The costs include labor, inventory, transportation, and overhead expenses.

**Task**: Discuss the challenges in accurately tracking and allocating costs in a fulfillment environment. Think about what are important considerations for this, what are previous experiences that you have you could related to this problem and elaborate some questions and considerations

**Questions you may have and considerations:**  
Cost Allocation & Tracking is one of the important & challenging thing for an organization, managing less would incur more cost and doing much may restrict the workers in challenging situation. Based on the team and nature of area, cost has to be managed. The important factor that should be kept in mind for any part should have,

**Distinguished Detail :**
In any team, the distinguished split of cost will help to see where it is spent more and one can be controlled or one can be allowed allocating more budget. split would be like below

Labour: Labour hours, storage area, orders fulfilled, orders/volume shipped etc..  
Inventory: Availability, Fulfillment vs demand, replenishing time, etc..,  
Transportation: Right number of inventory, demand vs shipping, etc.., 

**Time :**
For a retail business, time is an important factor to be kept in the account. Managing right time and momentum keep the business operating with expected cost. Delays in time vs expected, planned time management vs actual time consumed, are few considerations for time that can be evaluated and reported for a better Time Management.

**Location:**
Mantaining Stores & Warehouse is not a simple business area where cost vary much. All the business are ready to spend the shipping cost over location where the transit cost is less compared to having the warehouse in the city. Where as in case of Store it should be planned well for the customers to be able to reach, use effectively.

### Questions:

- What are the key decision stratergy that should be used/considered for the cost and how it should be reported?
- Are we evaluating Stratergy vs Actual Reporting periodically Weekly/monthly/Yearly?
- Is the evaluation being considered for planning & budgeting?
- Based on the practical and unforeseen challenges is there any reserved budget (Eg: Natual calamities like Covid breakout)?
